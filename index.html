About

Where education meets immigration, run by a founder who builds.

Get the right student into the right program, funded, and across the border.

Since 2022 I have built EdMission into an international education and immigration consultancy that guides students from first application to arrival abroad. Before Canada, I ran an overseas education consulting business in India.

My edge is operational. As a non-technical founder, I design, build, and run products through AI orchestration, directing models across research, reasoning, and execution so a small operation moves like a much larger one.

The result is a practice that pairs hands-on admissions expertise with AI-native operations.
